About AfterAI

Our Mission

We believe that in the age of AI, the most important thing isn't competing for results, but savoring the process.

Through handwritten newsletters, AI-powered learning tools, and community activities like event exhibitions, we're proposing ways to enjoy our lives as an adventure.

Our Story

Founded: Established in April 2023 as a solo venture by a software engineer, Yuji Tabata

Maker Faire Journey: We've been showcasing creative projects at Maker Faires in the US, Italy, and Japan, connecting with fellow makers and artists

Today: We build AI-powered products like babysteps and deliver handwritten newsletters through AfterAI Weekly

What Makes Us Different

  • 100% Handwritten Newsletters: Every issue is written by hand and carefully scanned
  • AI-Powered Products: Building tools like babysteps that make learning personal and adaptive
  • Global Community: From Silicon Valley to Tokyo, connecting curious people everywhere
  • Humanity-First: We focus on what's most important for humanity
